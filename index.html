-|-| R E i X E T A |-|-

The Reixeta project is based on the creation of a typeface and a typographic and visual editing system for creating messages from the typeface itself in bitmap format for each letter. Each part of the letter has been created by scanning a grid and using its own graphic structure to develop the architecture of its characters.

Reixeta is a visual system created from a structure that seeks coexistence between living and natural beings. The goal is protection between them. A technology that is designed as a mediator.

It's a work in progress, somewhere between the '80s aesthetic of early computers and the typographic creation and linkage of the idea of signs for garden beds, fences, makeshift doors, and personal ads...examples
